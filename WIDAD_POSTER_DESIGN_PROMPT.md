### 4. الأعمدة الثلاثة: Abstract / Problem Statement / Proposed Solution

**Abstract:**
```
Widad is an AI-powered digital health platform designed to support
women through every stage of their reproductive health journey —
from pre-marital health awareness, through pregnancy, to postpartum
motherhood care.

The platform integrates machine learning risk-prediction models,
a context-aware intelligent chatbot, and telehealth consultations
into one unified system accessible via web and mobile.

Using advanced AI models, Widad helps patients detect pregnancy-
related risks early, receive personalized health guidance, track
their health data continuously, and connect with certified doctors
— reducing reliance on scattered, disconnected healthcare touchpoints.
```

**Problem Statement:**
```
Traditional maternal healthcare in the region depends heavily on
periodic clinical visits, leaving women without continuous guidance
between appointments.

Challenges include:
• Late detection of high-risk pregnancy conditions
• Limited access to specialized doctors, especially in remote areas
• Lack of personalized, round-the-clock health guidance
• Fragmented tracking of health data across multiple channels
• Delayed consultations due to logistical & scheduling barriers
• No unified platform connecting AI insights with real doctor care
• Difficulty accessing lab test interpretation without a specialist
```

**Proposed Solution:**
```
Widad integrates:
✓ AI Prediction Models (GDM, Preeclampsia, Preterm Birth, SCBU)
✓ Context-Aware AI Chatbot (4 specialized modes)
✓ Telehealth Video Consultations (Google Meet integration)
✓ Health & Cycle Trackers (Weight, Mood, Period, Fertility)
✓ Lab Test OCR Analysis
✓ Google Fit Integration (Steps, Heart Rate, Sleep)
✓ Secure Online Payment & Booking System
✓ Doctor & Admin Management Dashboards
```
